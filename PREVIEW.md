## 🎨 **Color Scheme & Design**

### 🌈 **Primary Colors**
- **Gradient**: Purple-Blue (#667eea → #764ba2)
- **Background**: Clean whites and light grays (#f8f9fa)
- **Text**: Dark grays (#333) and medium grays (#666)
- **Accents**: Vibrant gradients for buttons and highlights

### 🔤 **Typography**
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display (elegant serif)
- **Body Text**: Inter (clean sans-serif)
- **Sizes**: Responsive from mobile (2rem) to desktop (4rem)

### 📱 **Responsive Features**
- **Mobile Navigation**: Hamburger menu
- **Grid Layouts**: Auto-adjusting columns
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large buttons and links
- **Optimized Images**: Proper sizing for all devices

## ⚡ **Interactive Features**

### 🎯 **Homepage Interactions**
- ✨ Smooth scroll animations on page load
- 🖼️ Hover effects on featured work images
- 📊 Animated counter for statistics

### 🔍 **Portfolio Filtering**
- 🏷️ Category buttons (All, Portraits, Weddings, etc.)
- 🎭 Fade animations when switching categories
